The Cherabuddi Education Society was registered in January 1999 as an NRI-promoted society. The Society aims at creating a state-of-the-art engineering institution in association with leading NRI technology professionals and well-known academicians of the twin states of Telangana & Andhra Pradesh. The Society aims to harness technical excellence with a commitment to the ethos of 'useful learning'

PLACEMENTS

Record Placements The college has been creating records year after year. In spite of a recession year with about 100 companies visiting CVR and above 1500 placements for the 2022-23 academic year, it is the highest among the peer group of colleges. The average salary increased significantly to a new high. The highest offer is a remarkable Rs. 57,50,000 and several students got offers higher than Rs. 25 Lakhs. More than 100 students got offers higher than Rs. 10 Lakhs, a new record. About 300 offers are higher than Rs. 6 Lakhs.

ATONOMOUS INSTIUTTION

CVR College of Engineering has been granted autonomous status by the UGC. CVR has set a record by getting this status in the shortest time (less than 12.5 years since the establishment) among 300+ colleges in the entire Telangana (Osmania University) area.
